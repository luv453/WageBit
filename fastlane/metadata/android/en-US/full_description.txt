WageBit is a local-first dashboard for people who think about savings as preserved labor.
Enter monthly income, monthly expenses, weekly and daily work hours, current BTC price, BTC lots with acquired dates, target expense years, cost-of-living increase, DCA amount, DCA frequency, current age, and BTC CAGR. The app calculates real hourly wage, current Bitcoin value, inflation-adjusted goal progress, work time stored, time efficiency, the BTC price needed for freedom today, projected age, a projected freedom date, a compounding expense calculator, a home screen widget, and a comparison chart against S and P 500, Nasdaq-100, and gold CAGR assumptions.
WageBit does not use network access, analytics, ads, or trackers. All values are entered by the user and stored locally on the device.
